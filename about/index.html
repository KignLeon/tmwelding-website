The Story

BUILT ON SKILL,
TRUST & HARD WORK

I'm Thai, the welder behind T & M Welding. For over 18 years, I've been doing metal work and welding for customers across San Diego. What started as a skill I learned young has turned into a full-time business built on word of mouth and repeat customers.

I specialize in MIG welding using Argon and CO₂ for clean, strong welds on all kinds of projects — from exhaust repairs and trailer fixes to custom BBQ grills and security gates. If it's metal and it needs welding, I can handle it.

"I believe in doing the job right the first time, charging a fair price, and treating every customer the way I'd want to be treated. No games, no upsells — just honest work."

T & M Welding is a small, local business. That means when you call, you talk to me. When I give you a quote, that's the price. And when I finish the job, you'll see the quality for yourself.
